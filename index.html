Active Effects
Rate

Multiplies your coin production by the shown value for the duration. Multiple Rate buffs stack multiplicatively — two ×3 buffs give ×9.

Luck

Adds the shown percent to your gamble win chance. Stacks additively across all active Luck buffs. A 1% gamble becomes 11% under a +10% Luck buff.

Cushion

Refunds the shown percent of your wager when a gamble loses. Stacks additively across active Cushion buffs, capped at 100% (full refund on loss).

Compound

Your production multiplier grows continuously over time, starting at ×1 and compounding every second. The shown value is the current multiplier — let it run longer for a bigger payoff.
