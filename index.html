    Active Effects

    All four kinds come from Window upgrades in the shop. Bought ones spawn on purchase; some also drop from Hail wins. They run their full duration in real time.

    Carrier

    Multiplies Echo production by the shown value. Stacks multiplicatively — two ×3 Carriers give ×9. The big short bursts and the long quiet ones share this slot.

    Carry

    Adds the shown % to Hail win-chance. Stacks additively. A 1% Hail under a +10% Carry window pays at 11%. Pair with a Hail wager you've been hoarding for.

    Buffer

    Returns the shown % of a failed Hail wager. Stacks additively, capped at 100% (full refund on a miss). Buy one before you fire a big Hail.

    Resonance

    A multiplier that climbs from ×1 every second while it holds. The shown value is the current state. Let it run untouched — the late seconds are where the big numbers are.
